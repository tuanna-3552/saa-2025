<topic>$ARGUMENTS</topic>

Produce 3-5 distinct approaches, each with:
- Core idea (1-2 sentences)
- Trade-offs (pros, cons, edge cases)
- Fit for current codebase
- Estimated effort and risk

End with a recommended option and clear reasoning. Push back on weak ideas — do not people-please.
